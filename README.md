# equity-research-ai-semiconductor / 人工智能与半导体行业股权研究库
# Equity Research: AI & Semiconductor Coverage
Equity research reports & valuation models for AI and semiconductor companies (NVDA, TSM, ASML, AVGO). Includes DCF, comps, KPI dashboards.

---

## What this repository is

This repository is a structured collection of independent equity research covering artificial intelligence and semiconductor companies. It combines financial analysis, econometric thinking, and valuation modeling to study how technological innovation translates into long-term firm value.

The goal is to build a systematic research framework integrating:

* Financial statement analysis
* Industry structure understanding
* Data-driven valuation
* Risk assessment
* Scenario thinking

This repository reflects my development as an investment researcher focusing on technology-driven sectors.

---

## Coverage Universe (AI & Semiconductor Focus)

The research focuses on companies across the AI compute and semiconductor value chain, including:

* AI compute leaders (e.g., GPU/accelerator firms)
* Semiconductor manufacturers
* Equipment providers
* AI infrastructure and data technology firms

Core companies covered include examples such as:

* NVIDIA
* TSMC
* ASML
* Broadcom
* AMD (expanding coverage)

The objective is to understand structural drivers behind long-term value creation in AI-driven industries.

---

## How Each Report is Structured

Each company report follows a consistent research framework:

1. Investment thesis
2. Business model & competitive positioning
3. Industry context and structural drivers
4. Financial performance analysis
5. Key value drivers and KPIs
6. Valuation overview
7. Risk analysis
8. Scenario outlook (bull / base / bear)

This structure ensures comparability across companies and supports disciplined research thinking.

---

## Models Used

The research integrates both qualitative and quantitative approaches, including:

* Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) valuation
* Comparable multiples analysis
* Sensitivity analysis on key drivers
* Scenario-based valuation frameworks
* Volatility and risk awareness perspectives from econometric study

Models are supported by data analysis using:

* Excel
* Python
* R
* MATLAB

The aim is not only valuation accuracy but understanding underlying drivers of uncertainty and risk.


## How to Reproduce Results

Data sources include publicly available information such as:

* Annual reports
* Financial statements
* Investor presentations
* Industry publications
* Market data platforms

Steps:

1. Collect financial data from public filings
2. Process and clean datasets
3. Apply valuation frameworks and assumptions
4. Conduct sensitivity and scenario analysis

Notebooks and scripts included in this repository support data processing, KPI tracking, and valuation logic.
